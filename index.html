The web

Paperweb.in


Prajwal Haniya founded Paperweb.in in 2019.A professional blog for writers. The main aim of the website/blog was to encourage writing skills among youth. Writing is an art, Not everybody can have the skill to express his/her ways through writing, it is only for the people with a specific taste in the art of writing.


This blog succesfully completed one year journey.It contained 100+ articles by several writers. S G Akshaykumar and Nithin Javgal being the major contributors.


The blog/website brought down after a span of one year. The reason behind bringing down the blog is the major mistakes made during the first phase. Google did not rank the page effectively no matter what the keywords were.


There were many other reasons behind bringing the website down. Those are the mistakes, which taught us a lot of lessons.
